It's super easy

Just download Zart from app store, lauch Zart, and touch the only button on the screen.

Select the interval

Zart can compose you songs weekly or monthly. The longer it takes to compose, the more interesting the song will be.

Give Zart some inspiration

Have complete control over where Zart draws its inspiration from.

Wait for your masterpiece

Zart will send you a notification to let you know when it's done composing a song.

Enjoy!
